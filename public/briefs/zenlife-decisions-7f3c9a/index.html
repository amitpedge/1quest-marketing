  • SEO & discoverability: marketing pages, search indexing, and app-store presence.
  • Referral & growth: invitations, referrals, and virality mechanics.
  • Financial operations: reconciliation, payouts, chargebacks, and tax filing.
  • Advanced analytics: experiments and cohort analysis once there is meaningful traffic.
